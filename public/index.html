Frequently asked questions

How does Stormy help with influencer marketing?

Stormy uses AI to match your brand with relevant creators across multiple platforms. You describe your ideal influencer in natural language, and our system analyzes millions of profiles to find the best matches based on content, audience demographics, and engagement patterns.

What platforms does Stormy support?

We currently support YouTube, TikTok, TikTok Shop, LinkedIn, Substack, Spotify Podcasts, and Twitch. We're continuously adding new platforms based on user demand.

How much does Stormy cost?

We offer a free tier with limited searches to get started. Paid plans start at $99/month with unlimited searches, automated outreach, and advanced analytics. Enterprise plans include dedicated support and custom integrations.

Can I automate my outreach campaigns?

Yes. You can create message templates, set up automated sequences, and schedule follow-ups. The platform tracks all interactions and response rates automatically.

How accurate is the AI matching?

Our AI achieves 95% match accuracy by analyzing creator content, audience demographics, engagement patterns, and brand alignment. The system improves over time as it learns from your campaign results.

Is Stormy suitable for small businesses?

Absolutely. Our platform is designed to scale with your business. Small teams benefit from automation that saves time, while larger organizations use advanced features like multi-campaign management and team collaboration tools.
